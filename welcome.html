Welcome Week

Welcome Week is a student's first interaction with university life and the time when college bonds are at their strongest. The JCR puts on many events throughout the week to ensure you can make the most of your first week at university and have a great time while making many new friends. We understand that starting university can be a daunting time, but we are here to help. Please check our FAQ page and Contact page if you have any questions or queries.
